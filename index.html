Place your controls

  1. Drag the round touchpad and the START button.
  2. Tap a control to adjust size & opacity, then tap Done.
  3. When ready, tap SET & START. You can re-open this anytime (⚙️).

This setup shows on every reload. RESET only restarts the game.
